Problem Definition / Background

Fragmented Feedback & Lost Insights.

Organizations receive feedback from internal forms, staff, and platforms like Google or Zomato. These are scattered across systems with no centralized visibility, making sentiment monitoring and timely responses impossible.

  • No Centralization: Reviews distributed across internal systems and 3rd party platforms.
  • Unstructured Collection: Lack of direct review collection mechanisms post-service.
  • Categorization Struggles: Difficulty filtering by food, service, staff, or ambience.
  • Delayed Action: Inconsistent responses impacting brand reputation and retention.
  • Blind Spots: No structured branch-wise or staff-wise performance insights.
